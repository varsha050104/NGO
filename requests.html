How It Works

  • Complete this form with accurate information about your needs
  • Our team reviews your request within 2-3 business days
  • We may contact you for additional information
  • If approved, we connect you with appropriate donors or NGOs
  • Receive assistance based on availability and match with donors

Tips for a Successful Request

  • Be specific about your needs and situation
  • Provide all required documentation to speed up processing
  • Be realistic about the assistance you're requesting
  • Answer all questions honestly and completely
